About Me

Hello! I am Professor Daniel, currently pursuing my career at the University of Dongguk as an Assistant Foreign Professor from Myanmar. I specialize in teaching International Trade, E-Commerce, Global Marketing, Trade Practice, Cultural Management, and Global Branding.
